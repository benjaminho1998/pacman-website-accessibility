About Me

World famous influencer and actor with a net worth estimated at $14 billion. Born in Japan in 1980. Lover of mazes and yellow pixel dots. Best friends with Blinky (red), Inky (light blue), Pinky (pink), and Clyde (orange).

Eight records in the Guinness World Records: Gamer's Edition 2008, including "Most Successful Coin-Operated Game".
